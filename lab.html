Forensics Mini-Lab

Finance employee clicked a fake invoice email and malware was installed. Review the evidence and choose the correct attack.

Scenario: A Finance staff member receives an email about an overdue invoice. Shortly after opening the attachment, their workstation exhibits suspicious activity. IT has collected artifacts. Inspect the evidence below.

From: accounts@micros0ft-billing.com
Reply-To: support@micros0ft-billing.com
Subject: URGENT: Overdue Invoice - Action Required
Attachment: Invoice_April_2026.docm

SPF: FAIL | DKIM: FAIL
Sender domain not in allowlist

.docm = macro-enabled. micros0ft uses "0" not "o".
